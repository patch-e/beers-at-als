Check-In
Tap a beer to view information about the beer from Untappd!

Tap the Check-In button to launch the Untappd app or Untappd website directly to the beer.
note: this feature is experimental and can sometimes result in a beer that isn't found on Untappd due to how it was named on the list.

Views
Tap a viewing style of your choice. The beers will automatically layout according to your selection. Viewing styles can be bookmarked directly for your convenience.

  • Table - the beers in tabular format.
    Tap column headings to sort. Smaller screens can swipe the table left and right to scroll horizontally.
  • List - the beers in vertical list format.
  • Block - the beers in block flow format.
    Larger screens can display more blocks horizontally at a time.

Filtering
Enter filter criteria in any of the beer lists. All fields are immediately filtered upon as you type. For example, type "IPA" and any beer name or style containing "IPA" will be found. The number of beers matching the current filter criteria is reflected in the number next to the filter input.
